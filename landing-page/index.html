Some random information.

The lion is a large, powerful carnivorous mammal known for its distinctive mane (in males) and its role as a top predator in the wild.

The elephant is the largest land animal, known for its massive size, long trunk, and tusks (in some species).

The rabbit is a small mammal known for its long ears, short fluffy tail, and fast reproductive rate.

The squirrel is a small to medium-sized rodent known for its bushy tail, sharp claws, and agile climbing abilities.
